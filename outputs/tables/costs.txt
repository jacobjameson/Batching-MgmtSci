=== CONSERVATIVE NATURE OF ESTIMATES ===

Excluded cost categories (would increase estimates):
  1. Non-significant imaging effects (US, CT): +~$68/patient
  2. Radiologist interpretation time
  3. Physician cognitive burden
  4. Patient time costs and lost productivity
  5. Downstream admission costs (40pp increase × ~$10K/admission)
  6. Incidental finding workup costs
  7. Radiation exposure costs
  8. Spillover congestion effects on other patients

  Imaging cost would be: $ 163  (vs. $ 77 )
